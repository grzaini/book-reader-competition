About BRC

BRC Vision

The aim of the Book Reader Competition (BRC), a non-profit association, is to encourage people, especially young people, for book-reading through establishing like these programs and by motivating them in desirable ways. It should be effective for the people living and carry out the community to the enlightenment. BRC with the target of essential and small communities want to provide his services later to the larger one depending on his budget and financial situation.

BRC Mission

  • Runs a competition for book readers every month and administers it in a good manner.
  • Encourages the readers by giving some gifts and in cases supports them financially.
  • Finds other opportunities for the readers to lead them ahead on their careers.
  • Provides additional useful books and other reading materials for the readers that through these they can achieve their goals and have their effects on the communities.
